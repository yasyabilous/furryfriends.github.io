Pet Hotel

Our Pet Hotel center gives the best care for your pet on the highest level. We provide cats with luxurious surroundings, Play Area including climbing jungle which they can explore all day long inside and out. Dogs receive regular exercises, playtime and a special heated pool on site so they can enjoy swimming.
Rest assured, your pet will feel safe and cozy with us!

Our service offers the convenience of picking up your dog or cat and returning them when you're available at home. Upon receiving your booking details closer to the date, we'll reach out via email or phone to confirm if you require pick-up assistance. If so, we'll gather your location and pick-up specifics. The process is identical for the drop-off service.
