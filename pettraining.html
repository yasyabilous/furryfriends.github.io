Pet Training

At FurryFriends, we believe in creating strong bonds between pets and their owners through our thorough cat and dog training services. Let us be your companions in helping your four-legged family members become well-behaved, confident, and happy pets for life.

Dog Training


Enroll your dog in our training program to help them become more obedient and well-mannered. During crucial socialization time with other dogs, your dog can also learn valuable commands and behaviors from our expert trainer partners thanks to our training program.
We charge €150 euro per session.

WHAT'S INCLUDED?
  • Basic Obedience
  • Important Commands
  • Leash Walking
  • Dog Socialization
  • Building Relationships With Your Dog

Service typically takes 1-2 hours.


Cat Training


Participate in our online cat training program to view and talk about typical cat behaviors, equipment, and settling in for a pleasant life with your feline companion.
We charge €80 euro per session.

WHAT'S INCLUDED?
  • Cat Behaviour
  • Advice on Play
  • Clicker Training
  • Toilet Training
  • General Training

Service typically takes 1 hour.
