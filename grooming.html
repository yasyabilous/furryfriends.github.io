At FurryFriends, we understand that every pet deserves to look and feel their absolute best, which is why we offer the best dog and cat grooming services tailored to meet the unique needs of your furry companion.

Dog Grooming


Our groomers provide gentle care and attention to make sure that your dog has a stress-free experience. With a focus on safety, comfort, and style, our dog grooming services will leave your dog feeling clean and looking their absolute best.
We charge between €40 to €90 euro per session.

WHAT'S INCLUDED?
  • Cut and Style
  • Nourishing Bath
  • Brush and Blow Dry
  • Nail Clip
  • Other Spa Services if Selected

Service typically takes 3-4 hours.


Cat Grooming


From luxurious baths using high-quality, cat-safe products to gentle brushing, we prioritize the comfort and well-being of your pretty cat throughout the grooming process.
We charge between €40 to €60 euro per session.

WHAT'S INCLUDED?
  • Mat Removal
  • Bath with Specialized Shampoos
  • Ear/eye cleaning
  • Brush and Blow Dry
  • Nail Clip
  • Finishing Coat Spray

Service typically takes 2 hours.
